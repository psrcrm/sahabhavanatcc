💬 WhatsApp Chatbot Flow

Automated ticket creation via WhatsApp Business API

🤖 Bot Conversation Flow
1
Resident sends any message Bot replies: "Welcome to SAHABHAVANA TOWNSHIP Helpline! 🏘️ Please type your flat number (e.g. B401)"
2
Resident sends flat number Bot validates against database and replies: "Hello [Name]! Select issue type:\n1. Plumbing 🔧\n2. Electrical ⚡\n3. Lift 🛗\n4. Security 🛡️\n5. Emergency 🚨\n6. Other"
3
Resident selects category Bot asks: "Briefly describe your issue (in English or Telugu):"
4
Resident describes issue Bot asks: "Would you like to add a photo? (optional) — Reply YES or SKIP"
5
Ticket Auto-Created Bot replies: "✅ Ticket #TKT-XXXX raised! Our team will contact you within 4 hours. Track status by typing STATUS"
6
Status Check Resident types "STATUS" anytime → Bot shows current status, assigned staff, ETA
